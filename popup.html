Time Span Calculator
  1. Go to api.slack.com/apps and sign in to your workspace
  2. Click Create New App → choose From scratch
  3. Give it a name (e.g. Time Tracker), select your workspace → Create App
  4. In the left sidebar under Features, click Incoming Webhooks
  5. Toggle Activate Incoming Webhooks to On
  6. Click Add New Webhook to Workspace
  7. Choose the channel to post to → click Allow
  8. Copy the webhook URL that appears and paste it in the field above
