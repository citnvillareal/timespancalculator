Time Span Calculator

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026  ·  Extension version 3.0

What data is collected

None. Time Span Calculator does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

How your entries are stored

Task names and time values you enter are saved using chrome.storage.local — Chrome's built-in local storage API. This data lives only on your device, inside your Chrome profile. It is never sent to any server, third party, or external service.

Network requests

The extension makes no network requests of any kind. It has no server, no analytics, no telemetry, and no ads.

Permissions

The extension requests one permission: storage. This is used solely to save your time entries between popup sessions so your work persists when you close and reopen the popup.

Clearing your data

Click Clear all inside the extension popup to erase all stored entries. You can also remove all data by uninstalling the extension from Chrome.
