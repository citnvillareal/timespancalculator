## Detailed description

Time Span Calculator is a lightweight popup for tracking how long you spend across tasks in a day — and sharing the results with your team.

ADD ENTRIES MANUALLY
Type a start and end time for any task and the duration is calculated immediately. Adjust either field at any time and the total updates on the fly.

USE THE BUILT-IN TIMER
Hit play on any entry to start a live timer. Pause it to bank the time, then resume or stamp a final end time when you're done. Multiple timers can run at the same time.

EDIT WHILE YOU WORK
Changed when you actually started? Edit the start time on a running timer and the elapsed count adjusts automatically. Need to correct a duration? Type it directly — "2h 30m", "1.5h", "90m", or "1:30" are all accepted — and the end time updates to match.

OVERNIGHT ENTRIES
Spans that cross midnight are handled automatically. A +1 badge marks any entry that ends the next day, and the duration is always calculated correctly. Click the badge to override it if needed.

SEND TO SLACK
Connect a Slack Incoming Webhook to post a formatted task summary — with each entry's time range, duration, and a session total — to any channel in one click. A built-in step-by-step guide walks you through creating a webhook if you haven't done it before.

SCHEDULED SENDS
Set the extension to automatically post your daily summary to Slack at a specific time. Choose recurring sends on configurable days of the week (Monday–Friday by default), or schedule a one-shot send on a specific date and time. If there are no entries when the scheduled time arrives, the send is silently skipped. Schedules fire even when the popup is closed.

DARK AND LIGHT MODE
The popup automatically matches your operating system's appearance. No setting to toggle — it just works.

PERSISTENT & PRIVATE
Your entries and webhook URL are saved locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. Nothing is sent anywhere except when you explicitly click Send to Slack. Close and reopen the popup at any time — everything is right where you left it.

COPY YOUR TOTAL
Click the total at the bottom of the popup to copy it to the clipboard.
